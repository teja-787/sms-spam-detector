Dataset Composition

The foundation of our project is the SMSSpamCollection dataset. While the majority of messages are legitimate 'ham', a significant portion are 'spam', creating an imbalanced dataset that presents a realistic challenge for our machine learning models.

The Language of Spam vs. Ham

Top Words in Ham Messages

Legitimate messages are personal and conversational, often using words like "u", "go", "get", and "ok".

Top Words in Spam Messages

Spam messages create urgency and entice action with words like "free", "call", "claim", and "text".

Message Length Tells a Story

A key distinguishing feature is length. Spam messages are often significantly longer than personal ham messages, as they contain detailed offers, instructions, and contact numbers.
